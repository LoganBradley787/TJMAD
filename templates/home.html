About Us

At the TJ Mobile Apps Club, we don't just strive to learn, we yearn to create. Every meeting brings a new idea to life and a new app one step closer to fruition. At our club, we aim to bring bright individuals together to make apps for a better tomorrow.




What We Do

NavTJ

No more getting lost. With NavTJ, you get an easy and helpful way to navigate the confusing halls of TJ. NavTJ ensures that you get to your destination quickly and hassle-free!

TJSeniorAssassin

TJ's Senior Assassin is an age old tradition, but that doesn't mean we need to use age old methods. TJ MAC designed an intuitive and aesthetic interface to facilitate the game, so that all participants and organizers can have a fun and enjoyable experience!
